The German Setting is the primary and longest-running storyline of the VortexFoods™ universe. It centres on a cast of characters living in or around a German university city. Stories follow multiple perspectives — students, professionals, retail workers, and aristocrats — in a world where VortexFoods™ shrinkies are a mundane fact of everyday life.

The setting begins with Take-out, which introduces the world through the eyes of both Julia Böhm and a shrunken perspective character named Felix. Over the course of 19 stories, the cast expands to include Vortex employees, aristocrats, Italian diplomats, paramilitary operatives, and British nobility.
