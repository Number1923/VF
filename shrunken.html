Victims & Prey
Shrunken Characters
This page documents named individuals who appear in the series in a shrunken state as VortexFoods™ products. Unlike normal-sized characters, these individuals have been reduced, packaged, and face — or have faced — consumption. Characters are grouped below by nationality. All entries on this page are characters with confirmed names. Anonymous victims are tracked in estimated kill counts on individual predator character pages.

Shrunken Americans
US nationals shrunken and packaged in the American Setting
114 Documented

American shrunken victims span five stories: Strange Behaviours, A Matter of Principle, Good for Goodness' Sake, Checking Out, and An Afternoon at the Pool. Their fates represent the full range of Vortex's selection criteria: economic marginalisation, political inconvenience, criminal justice outsourcing, activist suppression, and simple proximity to power. George Conway — a retired roofer on a German holiday — represents an anomalous case: a US national who ended up in a German Vortex box, the circumstances of which are left deliberately unexplained.
