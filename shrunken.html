Shrunken Characters
This page documents named individuals who appear in the series in a shrunken state as VortexFoods™ products. Unlike normal-sized characters, these individuals have been reduced, packaged, and face — or have faced — consumption. Characters are grouped below by nationality. All entries on this page are characters with confirmed names. Anonymous victims are tracked in estimated kill counts on individual predator character pages.

Shrunken Germans
German nationals intercepted, shrunk, and sold as Vortex product
17 Documented

German shrunken victims span five stories. Take-out: Felix, Yannik, Daniel, Christian Lange, and Nadine Kowalczyk — purchased in a single medium box by Julia Böhm. Live Feeding: the unnamed protagonist consumed by Amina Tijani. Beyond the Sea: the unnamed protagonist consumed by Lisa Mayer. The Study Session: Jim Thompson, Maike, and Markus — consumed at Lisa's study session. Quality Assurance: Lars, Bernd Zimmermann, Andreas, and Jörg Strobel — department managers and one criminal disposed of via Sabrina Kramer's lunch. Some victims appear in the American Setting despite being German nationals (Max, Thilo Busch, Heiko Brandt).

Notes on Victim Selection

VortexFoods™ shrinkies are assigned to packaging based on criteria that include criminal conviction, economic classification as a "net negative" (debt, unemployment, poor prognosis), and social deviance as defined by Vortex's institutional criteria. Some, like Timothy Walker, are formally sentenced criminals whose death sentences are carried out via Vortex. Others, like Megan Sanders, appear to be victims of politically motivated targeting — she was investigating journalist disappearances inconvenient to the Department of Justice and Vortex alike. Max and Thilo Busch represent an ideological/journalistic suppression category, handed over under the "Section V" protocol or equivalent international arrangements.
