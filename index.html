About the Universe

The VortexFoods™ universe is set in a world largely identical to our own, with one crucial difference: shrinking technology exists and is commercially available. The VortexFoods™ corporation has industrialised this technology, using it to shrink human beings and sell them as food products, marketed as "shrinkies" — living, conscious people reduced to a fraction of their original size and packaged in small plastic boxes for consumption.

Stories are told from both the perspectives of the shrunken people (prey) — experiencing terror, helplessness, and the horror of their situation — and the normal-sized characters (predators) — who largely view shrinkies as a mundane consumer product.
