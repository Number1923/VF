📦 VortexFoods™ Shrinkies
Wiki context: As depicted across the series — most prominently in Take-out, Live Feeding, Drive-through, and the American Setting stories — VortexFoods™ sells shrunken human beings as a consumer snack product in small plastic boxes. They are treated legally and culturally as a food item. Boxes vary by count. The individuals inside are alive, conscious, and aware of their situation. Names and origins of those inside are never printed on packaging.

Living, legal, and licensed. Each Vortex shrinkie is certified under the Federal Miniaturisation and Consumer Livestock Act. All box sizes are sealed, ventilated, and ready to enjoy. Age verification required at point of sale.
