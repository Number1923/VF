The canon guest stories introduce their own recurring characters while intersecting with the established VortexFoods™ universe. The Lefebvre sisters (Aurélie, Blandine, Florence, and Rosalie) are the most prominent recurring figures across the guest story catalogue, appearing in both The Rose and the Rebel and Hungry Skies arcs.

Several characters introduced in canon guest stories have physical profiles and biographical data that place them firmly in the VortexFoods™ world. Jutta Bauer, the German scientist from The Rose and the Rebel, is classified under the German Setting due to her nationality and connections.

Meryl Douglas, an American fitness coach appearing in Conversations with Cuisine, is also classified under the American Setting for the same reason.
